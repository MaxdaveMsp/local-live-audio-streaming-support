About the App

Local Live Audio Streaming lets you instantly connect to live audio channels at conferences, events, and multilingual venues. Simply scan a QR code or enter an IP address provided by your event host to join a room and start listening in seconds.

How to Use

  • QR Code: Point your camera at the QR code provided at the event to auto-connect.
  • Manual Entry: Enter the IP address and port or room number given by the host.
  • Room Selection: Choose from available language or session channels.
  • Listen: Audio streams live directly to your earphones.

Frequently Asked Questions

What do I need to connect?
You need to be on the same local network as the streaming host, or have the IP address provided by them.


Why can I not hear audio?
Make sure your device volume is turned up, you are connected to the correct room, and that the host stream is active.


Does the app work without internet?
Yes! The app is designed for local network streaming and does not require an internet connection.
