About the app

Local Live Audio Streaming lets listeners connect to live audio rooms at conferences, interpretation booths, guided events, and multilingual spaces. The app is optimized for fast room entry and stable playback over the same local network as the host system.

How it works

  • Scan a QR code provided at the venue to fill the room automatically.
  • Enter the room manually if the host gives you the room URL or address directly.
  • Select the right channel for your language, session, or listening zone.
  • Listen live with earphones or your device audio once the room is active.

Frequently asked

What do I need to connect?

You need to be on the same local network as the host system, or use the exact room address supplied by the event team.

 

Why can I not hear audio?

Check your device volume, confirm you joined the correct room, and make sure the event host has already started the stream.

 

Does it require internet?

No. The system is designed for local-network streaming and can operate without internet access when the event network is configured correctly.
