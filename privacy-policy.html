Introduction

Loud and Beyond ("we", "us", or "our") operates the Local Live Audio Streaming mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our App.

By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the App.

Information We Do Not Collect

Local Live Audio Streaming is designed with your privacy in mind. The App does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information. Specifically, we do not collect:

- Your name, email address, or any contact details

- Your device location data

- Your IP address or network identifiers

- Any audio content you listen to

- Any usage statistics or analytics data

- Any account credentials

How the App Works

The App allows you to connect to local audio streams at events and venues by scanning a QR code or manually entering an IP address provided by the event host. All audio streaming occurs entirely on the local network. No audio data or connection information is sent to our servers or any third party.

The IP address or QR code you use to connect is only held temporarily in device memory during your session and is never transmitted outside of your local network.

Local Network Access

The App requests access to your device local network in order to connect to audio streams. This permission is used solely for establishing the audio connection within your local network and for no other purpose.

Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. We do not share any data with third parties because we do not collect any data in the first place.

Children Privacy

Our App does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13. Since we collect no personal data at all, the App is safe for users of all ages.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
