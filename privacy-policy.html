Privacy

Built for local listening, not data collection.

Local Live Audio Streaming is designed for controlled local-network audio delivery at conferences, events, and interpretation environments. The app is intended to keep listener access simple while minimizing the amount of information handled by the product.

Introduction

Loud and Beyond ("we", "us", or "our") operates the Local Live Audio Streaming mobile application (the "App"). This policy explains how information is handled when you use the app.

By using the app, you agree to the practices described here. If you do not agree, do not use the app.

Information we do not collect

The app is designed to avoid collecting personal data. We do not collect or store:

  • Your name, email address, or contact details
  • Your location data
  • Your listening history
  • Audio content you listen to
  • Advertising identifiers or analytics tracking data
  • Account credentials for a user profile system

How the app works

The app connects listeners to authorized local audio rooms using a QR code, room URL, or network details supplied by the event host. The app is designed around room-based local streaming rather than public media access.

Room details used for connection are handled only to establish the session and are not intended to be transmitted to external analytics or advertising services.

Local network access

The app may require local network access so it can discover and connect to the event audio system. That access is used for local streaming connectivity only.

Third-party services

The app is not intended to integrate third-party advertising, tracking, or analytics services as part of the listening flow.

Children's privacy

The app is not designed to collect personal information from children. Since the product is structured to minimize personal data collection, it is intended to be safe for general event use across age groups.

Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any updates will be published on this page.
